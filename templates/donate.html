Other Donations

Non-perishable food or personal care items help support Franklin County and aides those we serve. If you wish to donate such items to Franklin County , please call us in advance so that we may make any necessary arrangements. Additionally, many businesses support Franklin County through gift-in-kind donations to specific fundraising events. By providing merchandise or services, businesses publicly show their care and concern for the communities in which they do business while also increasing awareness of their brand, product or service.
